Postdoc call – Yixin Wang's Group, UMich Statistics
Professor Yixin Wang is seeking applications for a postdoctoral researcher. The position is for a researcher to work on causal machine learning and/or probabilistic generative modeling. Causal inference for high-dimensional, unstructured data (e.g. image, text, video, molecule structure, gene expression) is of particular interest, but applicants with backgrounds in other areas of statistics/causal inference and machine learning will be considered.

The expected start date is February 1 or September 1, 2025 – other dates possible by mutual agreement. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis.
